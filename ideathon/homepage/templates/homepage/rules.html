Event Rules

  • 1.The ideathon is open to students of all disciplines, but participants must have a valid student ID.
  • 2.From a team, any one member can register.
  • 3.Participants can form teams of up to 5 members(min 2), per member 30/- rupees will be charged.
  • 4.After completing the registration process, we will provide login details via email to the team lead's email address. Through the login, the team can submit their PPT.
  • 5.Projects must be submitted through the online platform by the specified deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted..
  • 6. Follow the Code of Conduct provided by the organizers.
